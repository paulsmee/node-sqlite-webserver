Welcome to Hudson Science Laboratories

Here at HSL we provide backyard science to the world. Topics that are covered include Astronomy, Meteorolgy, Electonics and Robotics, Geology, Archeology and many more.
Our mission is to show the world what can be achieved with a basic level of knowledge and a drive to learn and create. Together we will learn about the various sciences and go on to discover new and exciting things. The world is out there waiting to be interacted with so lets get out there and do it.
